Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website for?

This site helps UVA students explore on-campus job opportunities using an interactive map that decentralizes hidden opportunities. It‚Äôs fast, visual, and requires no login.

How do I find jobs near specific locations?

Head to the map view, hover or click on a location pin to see jobs at that building. Job listings are grouped by on-campus addresses.

Can I save job reminders?

Yes! You can add custom reminders for job applications or interviews directly into your personal calendar. Everything is stored locally on your browser.

Do I need to log in?

No account or login required. Reminders and calendar events are stored safely on your device using local storage.

What can the chatbot do?

The chatbot helps you navigate the platform, answer job-related questions, explain filters, and assist with how to use each feature.

Who can I contact for support?

This is an open source project. You can find the contributors and their contact info on GitHub at CavHire repo.
